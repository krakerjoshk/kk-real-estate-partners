We buy apartment buildings in Northeast Ohio.

KK Real Estate Partners is a family-owned investment group based in Cleveland. We own and manage rental property here ourselves, and we buy 4–30 unit buildings as-is, with straightforward terms and a quick close — whether you're an owner selling directly or an agent bringing us a deal.

Talk to us about your building

Who we are

We're local, long-term owners — not a national wholesaler and not a lead-generation website. Through our management arm, 2040 Management, we operate rental property across Cuyahoga County, so we know what these buildings actually take to run: the water bills, the turnovers, the boiler that goes out in January.

When we reach out about a property, it's because we've already studied it — the neighborhood, the taxes, the rents — and we're prepared to make a real offer, not tie it up and reassign it.

What we buy

  • Apartment buildings and multifamily portfolios, roughly 4 to 30 units
  • Cleveland, Akron, and the surrounding Northeast Ohio counties
  • Any condition — deferred maintenance and needed repairs are fine
  • Tenants in place or vacant, month-to-month or leased
  • Complicated situations welcome: back taxes, code violations, estates, partnership exits, out-of-state owners done with long-distance management

How it works

Reach out

Call, text, or email. Tell us the address and whatever you're comfortable sharing about rents and condition. No obligation, and we keep it confidential.

We do the work

We walk the property on your schedule, run our own numbers, and come back quickly with a clear, written offer — no repair demands, no games. Represented by an agent? No problem — we work with brokers all the time.

Close on your timeline

We can move fast when you need speed, or work around tenants, taxes, and timing when you need flexibility.

Got a letter from us? That's really us — we send them to owners of buildings we'd genuinely like to buy. Reach out using the contact below, or call the number on your letter, and we'll tell you exactly what we can offer and why.

Agents & brokers

We buy through agents and brokers regularly, and we're the buyer you want on the other side of a tough multifamily listing: we're direct principals purchasing for our own portfolio, we buy as-is, and we close.

Your commission is safe

Bring us a deal and you stay in it. We honor buyer-side agreements and never go around a broker to the seller.

Fast, real answers

Send an address and rent roll and you'll get a yes, a no, or a number quickly — we won't sit on your listing or retrade at the finish line.

We take the hard ones

Deferred maintenance, inherited tenants, estates, expired listings, pocket listings — the deals other buyers pass on are often the ones we want.
